The Bot may store user data, including guild ID, guild's owner ID, and user IDs, for the purpose of enabling the Bot's functionality. We may also store non-personally identifiable data about the usage of the Bot, such as frequency of use and interactions with the Bot.

4. Disclosure of Information

We do not disclose any personally identifiable information collected by the Bot to third parties. We also do not disclose non-personally identifiable information to third parties.

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Any changes to the Privacy Policy will be effective immediately upon posting to our GitHub page.

Cancellation and Refund Policy

Cancellation and Refund

Order Cancellation

  • Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of purchase, provided we have not started working on the orders.
  • To request a cancellation, please contact us at refunds@nekocode.in.
  • If the work on your order has already been started, cancellation is not possible.

Refund Policy

  • Refunds are applicable only for orders that are placed by mistake.
  • Refund requests must be made within 7 calendar days of receiving the product.
  • Customers must provide a valid proof when requesting a refund.
  • Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days via the original payment method.

Delivery

We are committed to ensuring timely delivery of all orders. Please review our shipping and delivery terms:

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days after the payment confirmation.

Shipping Methods & Delivery Time

  • Standard Shipping: 5-10 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-5 business days

Shipping Charges

For the Express Shipping, an extra shipping fee is applicable. Free shipping is available for Standard Shipping option..

Order Tracking

Once the order has been completed and is about to be handed over to the customer, they will receive the confirmation and the next required steps via email.

Delivery Issues

  • If an order is delayed, please contact us at orders@nekocode.in with your order details.
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by unforeseen circumstances (e.g., weather, strikes).

Contact Us

For any inquiries, support, or assistance, please reach out to us:

  • Email: contact@nekocode.in
  • Support Hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM IST
